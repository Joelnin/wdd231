Discover the Wild

Site Name: Discover the Wild

The name “Discover the Wild” encapsulates the essence of the website by inviting users to explore and learn about the natural world. It sparks curiosity and encourages a sense of adventure. Also, it aligns with our mission to educate and promote wildlife awareness.

Site Purpose

The purpose of Discover the Wild is to educate, engage, and inspire visitors by providing detailed and interactive information about animals and their classifications. The website serves as a comprehensive resource for nature enthusiasts, educators, and students.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: "I'm a parent looking for educational resources for my kids to learn about animals in a fun way. Does this site have interactive features or easy-to-understand content?"

    The website offers user-friendly, interactive features such as filtering animals by diet, habitat, or reproduction and saving favorite animals, making it engaging and suitable for children.

  • Scenario 2: "I'm curious about the diet of specific animals. Can I search for herbivores, carnivores, or omnivores on this site?"

    Yes! Users can filter animals based on their diet, allowing them to explore herbivores, carnivores, and omnivores easily.
